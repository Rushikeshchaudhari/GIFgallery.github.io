Wire Cutter
Wire cutters are commonly used to cut copper, brass, iron, aluminum, and steel wire.
Linemen's Wire Cutters
They are also known as combination wire cutters. The fact that these wire cutters have a sharp cutting edge along with a firm surface to grip gives them the name of Combination Wire Cutters.
Locking Wire Cutters
Carpenters use a lot of locking wire cutters. As the name rightly suggests, these wire cutters have an edge over other normal ones .
